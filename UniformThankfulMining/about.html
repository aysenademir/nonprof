Our Story

Together for Tomorrow’s Kids: Founded by Ayse Sena Demir, a High School Sophomore, with a Mission to Help Kids in Need.

Ayse Sena Demir lived in Uganda for several years, where she witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by children in need. Inspired by her experiences, Ayse has dedicated herself to helping others through charitable initiatives.

From a young age, Ayse volunteered at various charity events across Uganda. She participated in local food drives, assisting in the distribution of essential supplies to families facing hardships. Ayse also worked alongside fellow volunteers in orphanages, supporting children who often faced profound emotional and physical challenges. Her time spent with these children deepened her understanding of their needs and the importance of fostering a nurturing environment.

Through her hands-on experiences, Ayse witnessed the resilience and strength of the young lives she encountered. Motivated by a desire to make a lasting impact, she felt a strong calling to create something meaningful. This inspiration led her to establish Together for Tomorrow’s Kids, a nonprofit dedicated to supporting children in need. Though the organization is still in its early stages, Ayse’s commitment to making a difference remains unwavering.

Through her experiences in Uganda, Ayse has gained valuable insights into the needs of children and families, fueling her mission to create a brighter future for all. With Together for Tomorrow’s Kids, she looks forward to channeling her passion and the lessons she’s learned into meaningful support for those in need.

The founder and volunteers of Together for Tomorrow’s Kids are passionately dedicated to helping children in need, striving to create a brighter future for every child.

Our Mission

“At Together for Tomorrow’s Kids, we are a newly established nonprofit driven by the belief that every child deserves a bright future. Our mission is to empower and uplift children around the world through access to education, health resources, and opportunities that spark their potential. We aim to unite communities and mobilize support to ensure that every child can dream, thrive, and create a better tomorrow. As we embark on this journey, we are committed to building a global network that nurtures potential and champions the rights of every child, fostering a world where hope knows no boundaries.”
